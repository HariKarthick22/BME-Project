# MediOrbit User Guide

## What is MediOrbit?

MediOrbit is an AI-powered medical tourism assistant that helps patients find the best hospitals in Tamil Nadu, India for their medical needs. It uses artificial intelligence to understand your medical requirements and match you with hospitals that are the perfect fit.

## Getting Started

### Home Page

When you first open MediOrbit, you'll see:
1. **Welcome message** with an introduction
2. **Chat widget** floating at the bottom right
3. **Quick search suggestions** as chips (tags)
4. **Animated steps** showing how to use the service

### Your First Search

#### Method 1: Using Chat

1. Click the **blue chat bubble** at the bottom right
2. Type your medical need:
   - "I need knee surgery in Chennai"
   - "Looking for cardiac treatment in Coimbatore with budget of ₹5L"
   - "Orthopedic specialist near Madurai"
3. Press **Enter** or click **Send**
4. MediOrbit will:
   - Understand your needs
   - Find matching hospitals
   - Show you the best options

#### Method 2: Using Quick Search Chips

1. Look at the suggested quick searches
2. Click one of the chips (e.g., "Cardiac Surgery")
3. You'll be taken to results with relevant hospitals

#### Method 3: Upload a Prescription

1. Click the **prescription upload button** at bottom left
2. **Drag and drop** your prescription image/PDF or click to select
3. MediOrbit will:
   - Extract medical details via OCR
   - Identify your diagnosis and procedures
   - Match hospitals for your procedure
4. Review the extracted information
5. Click "Find Hospitals" to see recommendations

## Search Results Page

### Understanding the Results

Each hospital card shows:
- **Hospital name and city**
- **AI Score** (visual ring, 0-10)
- **Starting price** in lakhs (₹5L = ₹500,000)
- **Success rate** percentage
- **Savings vs market average** percentage
- **Specialties** (as tags)
- **View Hospital** button to see full details

### Filtering Results

#### Available Filters

1. **Specialty Filter**
   - Search for specific medical specialties
   - Options: Cardiology, Orthopedics, Neurology, Oncology, etc.
   - Select multiple to refine search

2. **City Filter**
   - Choose hospitals by location
   - Options: Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Salem, etc.
   - Helpful for proximity

3. **Minimum Rating Filter**
   - Slide to set minimum rating threshold
   - Or use preset ratings (3.5+, 4.0+, 4.5+)
   - Ensures quality hospitals

4. **Sort Options**
   - **Rating (High to Low)**: Best-rated first
   - **Cost (Low to High)**: Most affordable first
   - **Distance (Low to High)**: Nearest first

### Viewing Results

- **Grid View**: Multiple hospitals shown together (default)
- **Scroll**: Infinite scroll loads more results
- **Click on Card**: Opens full hospital details page

### Clear Filters

Click the **"Clear Filters"** button to reset all filters and start fresh.

## Prescription Upload

### How to Upload

#### Method 1: Drag and Drop
1. Click the prescription upload area
2. **Drag** your prescription image from your computer
3. **Drop** it to upload
4. Wait for processing

#### Method 2: Click to Select
1. Click **"Select File"** button
2. Choose image from your device
3. Click **"Open"**

### Supported Formats

- **Images**: JPG, PNG, JPEG, GIF, WebP
- **Documents**: PDF (with searchable text)
- **Size**: Up to 10 MB

### Processing

Processing takes 3-5 seconds:
1. Image is sent to backend
2. OCR extracts text from image
3. Medical NER identifies key information
4. Results are displayed

### Extraction Results

MediOrbit extracts:
- **Diagnosis**: What condition you have
- **Procedures**: Recommended treatments
- **Medications**: Prescribed medicines
- **Patient Info**: Age, gender (if available)

### Next Steps After Upload

After successful upload:
1. Review extracted information
2. Click **"Find Hospitals"** to search for specialists
3. Or click **"Upload Another"** to process another file

## Search Tips & Tricks

### Get Better Results

1. **Be Specific**
   - Instead of: "I'm sick"
   - Say: "I need knee replacement surgery"

2. **Mention Location**
   - Specify your city for targeted results
   - "In Chennai" narrows down options

3. **Include Budget**
   - "With budget of ₹5 lakhs" helps filter
   - Avoids showing unaffordable options

4. **Check Insurance**
   - Mention if you have insurance
   - Hospital accepts specific schemes

### Understanding Results

**AI Score (Match Rank)**
- 8.0-10.0: Excellent fit for your needs
- 6.0-7.9: Good match
- 4.0-5.9: Fair match
- Below 4.0: Limited match

**Success Rate**
- 95%+: Excellent outcomes
- 90-94%: Very good
- 85-89%: Good
- Below 85%: Check reviews

**Cost Comparison**
- "-50%" means 50% cheaper than market average
- Useful for finding value

## Troubleshooting

### Chat Not Responding

**Problem**: Typed message but no response

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Wait 10 seconds (AI is thinking)
3. Try simpler message
4. Refresh page and try again

### Hospital Not Found

**Problem**: Can't find a specific hospital

**Solutions**:
1. Try different spelling
2. Search by broader specialty
3. Try city name only
4. Check if hospital name is correct

### Prescription Not Processing

**Problem**: Upload failed or no results

**Solutions**:
1. Check file format (JPG, PNG, PDF)
2. Ensure file is < 10 MB
3. Image should be clear and readable
4. Try another image quality

### Filters Not Working

**Problem**: Filters don't seem to affect results

**Solutions**:
1. Click "Clear Filters" first
2. Apply one filter at a time
3. Refresh page
4. Try different filter combination
